Falafal & Humus

Yields - 4 Servings
Cook Time - 20min

Ingredients

FALAFAL
• ½ Onion chopped finely

• 3-4 Garlic cloves
• ½ tsp Cumin powder
• ¼ tsp Organic Red chilli powder
• 2 tsp Organic Besan
• 3-4 tbsp Coriander leaves with stem
• 1 Ginger
• ¼ tsp Organic Coriander Powder
• Pinch of Cardamom powder
• 1 cup Organic Chickpeas soaked overnight
• Salt to taste


HUMUS
• 200 gm of boiled Organic Chickpeas
• 2 tbsp tahini (sesame paste)
• ¼ tsp Cumin powder
• Juice of 2 lemons
• 2 Garlic cloves
• 2-3 tbsp Olive oil
• Finely chopped green chili
• Salt to taste

Instructions

Falafal
1) Except besan, grind all the ingredients together in a mixer.
2) Add besan to the grounded mixture.
3) Make balls out of this mixture and deep fry. Keep the falafel balls aside.

HUMUS
1) Grind all the ingredients to make a smooth paste.
2) If needed, add some water to dilute this paste. Pour it in a serving bowl and add olive oil.
3) Serve with Falafels.
